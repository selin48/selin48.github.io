Web Host Assignment

Bluehost

This host looks promising, as it has high uptime and fast loading speed, generous storage, reliable security, and free SSL certificate along with many more features. The costs vary depending on which plan you want. People building personal resume sites or professional blogs, business owners of local enterprises and online stores, users of WordPress, Joomla, and Drupa should consider this web host.

IONOS

This host is best for startups. It has very fast page loading time(1.23s) and is very cheap, starting at $1 for 6 months and then $12/month.The data you have is mirrored in two data centers, so your hosting stays uninterrupted even during maintenance and outages.

HOSTGATOR

This host starts at $3.75/month. It has technical support at all time, gives free SSL certificate, unmetered bandwidth, and very easy installs.

GoDaddy

This host is best for easy site set up. The prices start at $3.74/month.It has easy to use control panel, can install over 150 free apps with one click, 24/7 security monitoring and DDoS, free domain, and gives good storage.

My favorite

My favorite is the Bluehost because it has features close to what I think would be useful.
